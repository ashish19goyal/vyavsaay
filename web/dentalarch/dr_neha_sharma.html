Dr. Neha Sharma Oral & Maxillofacial Surgeon
B.D.S. | M.D.S. Oral and Maxillofacial Surgeon | Senior Resident, Head & Neck Oncology
Dr. Neha started her path in dentistry after graduating from Rajeev Gandhi university of health sciences in 2011. She completed her master's in Maxillofacial Surgery from Manipal university in 2015.

She expertises in -
  • Painless Tooth extraction
  • Wisdom tooth removal
  • LASER assisted surgeries
  • Biopsies
  • TMJ related disorders
  • Jaws fractures and Reconstruction
  • Oral cancer screening and treatment
  • Implants under General anaesthesia
  • Wisdom tooth removal under General anaesthesia
As a part of her social responsibility she is involved in various cancer screening Camps and educating society towards adverse effects of tobacco.
