Dr. Anish Goyal Prosthodontist
B.D.S. | M.D.S. (Prosthodontics Crown & bridge) | P.G.C.A.O.I. (Implants Manipal) | P.G.C.C. (LASER DENTISTRY) | Date (Manipal)
Dr. Anish started his dental practice in 2010 after graduating from kUk university with a BDS DEGREE. He pursued his master's in Prosthodontics and oral implantology from Manipal university Manipal. He obtained his postgraduate certification in LASER DENTISTRY in 2014 from Manipal university. He has been actively involved in teaching in recognized dental university in Delhi NCR since 2015.

His area of expertise are -
  • Esthetic Crown and Bridge.
  • CAD Cam crowns and Veneers
  • Single and multiple Implants
  • Fixed Dentures
  • LASER surgeries
He is consulting Implantologist and Laser Specilaist in Multiple reputed hospitals and Dental practices in Delhi NCR. He is involved in various School Dental health programmes and Free dental Campus as part of his social responsibility.
