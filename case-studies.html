Uber for groceries

  • Description

    Take grocery store to mobile. Easily create a marketplace by adding more sellers. The solution has two mobile apps one is for customers and another one is for sellers and for people involved in order fulfillment. Customers can see feed of products, place an order and track the order. Sellers can see and manage their orders, send push notifications and messages automatically. Sellers can handle all of their business through the mobile app itself. All the options to manage orders, update inventory and product data, update delivery slots and adding employees are there. Everything is highly customizable.
